Sustainable student transportation

EcoRide focuses on reducing fossil-fuel travel, supporting shared mobility, and encouraging students to choose cleaner transport.

Why sustainability matters

Transport emissions and congestion are major challenges in urban areas. EcoRide responds by using electric vehicles and shared routes, reducing reliance on private fuel-powered cars for student travel.

  • Electric fleet positioning
  • Shared rides for lower congestion
  • Campus-focused pickup points
  • Eco-conscious student campaigns
